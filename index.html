Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba!

This is the coolest Anime about Demons and Demon Slayers. Our Main Character is Tanjiro Kamado who is on a journey to fight demons and become the greatest Demon Slayer while protecting his sister, Nezuko

After a demon attack leaves his family slain and his sister cursed, Tanjiro embarks upon a perilous journey to find a cure and avenge those he's lost.

The Hashiras
